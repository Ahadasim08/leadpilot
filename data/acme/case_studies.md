# Acme Digital — Case Studies

## RealCo Properties — Google Ads
RealCo, a residential property agency, came to us with a cost per lead of $85 and inconsistent lead quality from their Google Ads. We rebuilt their campaign structure around high-intent local keywords, rewrote ad copy against their top listings, and added proper conversion tracking. Within 3 months, cost per lead dropped 40% to $51, and monthly qualified leads rose from 34 to 61 on the same ad budget.

## Hartley & Shaw Legal — Local SEO
A two-partner law firm invisible in local search for their practice areas. We ran a technical audit, rebuilt their service pages, and executed a 6-month local SEO campaign including citations and review strategy. By month 6 they ranked in the top 3 map results for 8 of their 10 target terms, and organic inquiries grew from roughly 5 to 22 per month.
