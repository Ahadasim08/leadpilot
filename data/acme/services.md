# Acme Digital — Services

## PPC / Google Ads management
We plan, build, and manage Google Ads campaigns end to end: keyword research, campaign structure, ad copywriting, landing page recommendations, conversion tracking setup, and ongoing monthly optimization. This service is built for businesses already spending $2,000 or more per month on ads who aren't happy with their cost per lead. Clients typically see lower cost-per-lead and higher lead volume within the first 60–90 days.

## SEO
We handle technical SEO audits, on-page optimization, content strategy, and link building. Best suited to businesses that rely on local or industry search visibility — real estate, legal, healthcare, home services. SEO is a compounding channel: we set expectations of meaningful ranking movement in 3–6 months, not weeks.

## Web design & development
We design and build fast, conversion-focused websites on modern stacks. Typical projects are 5–10 page marketing sites and small e-commerce stores. Every build includes mobile optimization, basic on-page SEO, and analytics setup. We do not build large custom web applications.

## Social media management
We manage content calendars, post creation, scheduling, and community engagement for Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Best for brands that need a consistent, professional presence without hiring in-house. Paid social campaigns can be added and are billed under PPC management.
