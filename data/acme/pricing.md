# Acme Digital — Pricing

All prices in USD. Ad spend is always paid directly by the client to the ad platform and is separate from our fees.

## PPC / Google Ads management: $2,500–5,000/month
- Ad spend under $10,000/month → $2,500/month management fee
- Ad spend $10,000–30,000/month → $3,500/month
- Ad spend over $30,000/month → $5,000/month
- One-time setup fee: $500 (campaign build, tracking, first-month strategy)
- Minimum commitment: 3 months

## SEO retainer: $1,500–3,000/month
- Local SEO (single location): $1,500/month
- Regional/national SEO: $2,500–3,000/month
- Minimum commitment: 6 months (SEO results compound; shorter engagements waste money)

## Website design & build: $4,000–10,000 one-time
- 5-page marketing/brochure site: $4,000
- 10-page site with blog and custom design: $6,500
- Small e-commerce store: from $8,000
- Timeline: 4–8 weeks depending on scope
- Includes: mobile optimization, basic on-page SEO, analytics setup, 30 days of post-launch support

## Social media management: $1,200–2,500/month
- 3 posts/week, one platform: $1,200/month
- 5 posts/week, up to three platforms: $2,500/month
- Paid social ad management is billed separately under PPC rates
