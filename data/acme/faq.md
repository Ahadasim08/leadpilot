# Acme Digital — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the minimum contract length?**
A: 3 months for PPC and social media management, 6 months for SEO. Website builds are one-time projects with no retainer required.

**Q: How long does onboarding take?**
A: 1–2 weeks from signed agreement to campaigns live. Week one covers access, tracking setup, and strategy; week two is build and launch.

**Q: Who owns the ad accounts and website?**
A: You do, always. We work inside your Google Ads account and your hosting. If we part ways, everything stays with you — no hostage situations.

**Q: What industries do you work with?**
A: Primarily real estate, legal, healthcare, home services, and B2B services. We generally don't take gambling, crypto, or supplement clients.

**Q: How do you report results?**
A: A live dashboard you can check anytime, plus a monthly report call covering spend, leads, cost per lead, and next month's plan.

**Q: Is ad spend included in your fees?**
A: No. Ad spend is paid by you directly to Google or Meta. Our fee covers strategy and management only, so there's never a markup hidden in your media budget.
